Shell Renewables — M&A integration playbooks

Assistant Chief Data Officer / Data & integration architecture (Oct 2021 – Apr 2022)

Context

Rapid acquisition pace + complex landscape created friction for due diligence and integration.

What I did

  • Developed acquisition categories to shape due diligence and business intent.
  • Built a categorized set of due diligence + integration activities per acquisition type.
  • Created integration plans per category using stakeholder inputs (workshops/interviews).
  • Provided steer for target end-state process, data, and systems architecture post integration.

Outcome

  • Targeted due diligence and reduced unnecessary effort (e.g., avoiding deep code reviews when not required by acquisition type).
  • Tailored integration plans by category, improving speed and governance fit.
  • Improved decision clarity for leadership by linking integration effort to business intent.

Airbus — data hub benchmark to inform RFI decisions

Senior IT Architect / Platform strategy (May 2022 – Dec 2022)

Context

Airbus planned an RFI for data product management & interoperability, but needed clarity on which solutions aligned with its vision.

What I did

  • Designed an assessment framework for data hub solutions and tech stack combinations.
  • Benchmarked strengths, limitations, and trade-offs across solution options.
  • Facilitated workshops with vendors and aligned findings with Airbus stakeholders.
  • Provided first-level guidance to Airbus architects to raise maturity before RFI launch.

Outcome

  • Benchmark insights used by decision makers and procurement to select solutions to include in the RFI.
  • Created a shared evaluation language across stakeholders (architecture + procurement + delivery).

Rijkswaterstaat — architecture governance & PSAs in a large program

Solution Architect (May 2023 – Feb 2025)

Context

Major public-sector program with multiple initiatives requiring alignment with architecture, security expectations, and operational governance.

What I did

  • Delivered Project Start Architectures (PSAs) for critical information/platform initiatives.
  • Facilitated stakeholder workshops to align on assumptions, scope boundaries, and governance.
  • Assessed solutions against functional and security requirements and improved decision clarity.

Outcome

  • Validated architecture assumptions needed for security reviews and governance decision-making.
  • Reduced ambiguity on what is in/out of scope and who decides what (operating model clarity).
