### Enterprise Architecture: Governing the Modern Socio-Technical Enterprise
*Status: In development*

**Abstract:** Enterprise Architecture is the discipline that governs the modern socio-technical enterprise by aligning institutional intent, digital design, and operational reality. This article explores how organizations operate as complex systems across physical, digital, and institutional domains, and why architectural governance becomes essential in an age of encoded organizational logic and AI-driven decision-making.

**Key Themes:**
- Viewing enterprises as socio-technical systems
- How organizational logic gets encoded in software
- Making trade-offs visible in large organizations
- Architecture as a governance discipline
- AI's impact on architectural responsibility
